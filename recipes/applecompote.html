Warm Apple Compote

Description

Nothing says fall like a warm apple compote. A mixure of apples and spices slowly simmering can unravel anyone's senses and put them in an autumn mood. Plus its a good recipe to put all those apples from your apple picking adventures to good use. Served over ice cream, yogurt, on warn cinnamon toast, this compote can make any snack better.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Gala, Fiji or Honeycrisp apples
  • 1 Tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

  1. In a medium non-reactive pan saucepan (stainless steel, glass, or ceramic), combine the apples, brown sugar and lemon juice over medium heat.
  2. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes until the apples are tender.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in salt. Set aside and allow to cool.
  4. Serve any way you'd like!
