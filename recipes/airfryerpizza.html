Airfryer Cheese Pizza

Description

Quick and easy weekend meal event he kiddos can make themselves. Personal pizza you can add any toppings to to make your own.

Ingredients

  • 2(8-oz)packages of pizza dough
  • 1 tbsp evoo
  • 1/3 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. oregano
  • kosher salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • basil leaves, for serving

Ingredients

  1. On a clean, floured surface, gently flatten ball of dough with your hands until about 8” in diameter (or roughly smaller than your air fryer basket). Repeat with second dough ball. Brush both with olive oil and transfer one, oil side up, into the basket of your air fryer.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir to combine crushed tomatoes, garlic, and oregano, and season with salt and pepper. Spoon half tomato mixture onto the center of rolled out pizza dough, then spread into an even layer, leaving ½" outer crust bare.
  3. Add half the shredded mozzarella to pizza. Air fry on 400° for 10 to 12 minutes, or until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  4. Remove first pizza from air fryer basket using 2 pairs of tongs, and garnish with basil leaves. Assemble and cook second pizza, garnish, and serve.
